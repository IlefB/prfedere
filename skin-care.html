What is my skin type?

DRY

Flaky,scaly or rough patches on the face and /or body .

OILY

Shiny,greasy,looking most likely to have visible enlarged pores .

COMBINATION

Drier in some places (mostly cheeks) and oily in others such T-zone.

SENSITIVE

May burn or itch after using certain cosmetics and skincare products.

NORMAL

Blaces clear and not sentive .

HYPER-pigmented

Darker patches on face and/or body due to excess of melanin.
